Free Patern To Use

Patern For Tulips

1. Make a magic ring, and 6 single crochet into the ring that is the end of round 1.

2. Chain 1, Increase to each stitch. Now you have 12 stitches and that is the end of round 2.

3. Chain 1, 1 single crochet and 1 increase Repeat 6x, total of 18 stitches and that is the end of round 3.

4. Chain 1, 1 increase and 2 single crochet Repeat 6x, total of 24 stitches and that is the end of round 4.

5. Chain 1, single crochet to all stitches total of 24 stitches and that is the end of round 5.

6. For round 6-16, single crochet to all stitches until you reach round 16.

7. Cut about 5 inches yarn tail and add fiber fill to the tulip.

8. Sew the last part and hide the extra tail inside. Then you have a beautiful Tulips. This is called "the cross stitches technique". Visit my facebook page to learn to learn more.

Patern For Lily

1. Make a magic ring, and 6 single crochet into the ring and that is the end of round 1.

2. Increase single crochet total of 12 stitches, and that is the end of round 2.

3. 1 single crochet, 1 increase. Repeat 6x total of 18 stitches and that is the end of round 3.

4. For round 4-7 make 1 single crochet to each stitch and that is the end of round 7.

5. Make 1 single crochet, 1 decrease, Repeat 6x. Total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 8.

6. Make 1 single, 1 half double and 1 double crochet (same stitch). Next stitch 1 double, 1 half double, and 1 single crochet (same stitch). Repeat 3x and that is the end of round 9.

7. Slip stitch to secure the yarn, this is called "hide me tecnique." Visit my Visit my facebook page to learn to learn more.

Patern For Sunflower

1. Make a magic ring, 16 double crochet into the ring and slip stictch to the 1st stitch and that is the end of round 1.

2. To make the petals, change the color of your yarn to yellow then slip stitch to the 1st stitch. 1 double, 1 treble crochet (same stitch). Chain 2, slip stitch on the 1st of treble crochet, after that 1 treble and 1 double crochet (same stitch). Then slip stitch to the next stitch and that is the end of round 2.

3. Repeat round 2 until you encircle the whole flower. Sew the extra yarn on the petal of the flower and that is the end of round 3.

Patern For Daisy

1. Make a magic ring and 6 single crochet to the ring and that is the end of round 1.

2. Chain 1 Increase single crochet, Repeat 5x and then slip stitch. Total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 2.

3. Make single crochet to each stitch slip stitch to finish, Cut and leave a small tail yarn and that is the end of round 3.

4. Slip stitch for your new color of yarn and make 7 chain into the first stitch. From the 3rd chain make 1 half double to the next 4 stitch and to the last stitch make 1 single crochet. That is the end of round 4.

5. For the rest of the stitch, repeat round 4. Sew the end of extra yarn and that is the end of round 5.

Patern For Teddy Coaster

1. Make a slip knot and chain 3 and 10 half double crochet into the 1st chain. Slip stitch to join the round and that is the end of round 1.

2. Chain 2, and make 2 half double crochet in each the stitches. Slip stitch to join the round, you have total of 20 stitches by the end of the round and that is the end of round 2.

3. Chain 2, make 1 double crochet on the 1st stitch and 2 half double crochet on the next stitch. Repeat this pattern until the end of the round slip stitch to join the round, you should have 30 stitches and that is the end of round 3.

4. For the next round were not making a chain instead make 5 double crochet into the 1st stitch, this will form the ear of the bear. After that slip stitch to the next stitch and make 4 single crochet to next 4 stitches, slip stitch make 5 double crochet again and slip stitch to finish the ear. Single crochet to all the remaining stitches slip stitch and that is the end of round 4.

Patern For Bunny

1. Make a magic ring and 6 single crochet to the ring, total of 6 stitches and that is the end of round 1.

2. Increase single crochet and slip stitch to the 1st stitch, total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 2.

3. Make 1 single crochet and 1 increase, repeat 6x and total of 18 stitches and that is the end of round 3.

4. Make 2 single crochet and 1 increase, repeat 6x and total of 24 stitches and that is the end of round 4.

5. For round 5-7 make sigle crochet to all the stitches total of 24 stitches each round and that is the end of round 7.

6. Make 12 decrease total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 8.

7. Making a front loop (3 single, 1 bobble stitch, 4 single, 1 bobble stitch, and 3 single crochet) total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 9.

8. Make 1 single and 1 increase, reoeat 6x, total of 18 stitches and that is the end of round 10.

9. Make single crochet to all the stitches, total of 18 stitches and that is the end of round 11.

10. Making another front loop (1 bobble and 5 single crochet) repeat 6x and that is the end of round 12.

12. Make 1 single crohet and 1 decrease, repeat 6x and total of 12 stitch. cut the yarn and hide the extra yarn inside the bunny. That is the end of round 13.

Pattern For The Bunnies Ear

1. Make a magic ring and 6 single crochet inside the ring and that is the end of round 1.

2. Increase stitches total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 2.

3. Make single crochet to all the stitches, total of 12 stitches and that is the end of round 3.

4. Make 1 single and 1 decrease repeat 4x total of 8 stitches and that is the end of round 4.

5. Make single crochet to all the stitches total of 8 stitches and that is the end of round 5.

6. The final step make 4 single crochet to the side of the ear and sew the extra yarn. Sew the parts together and you have a beautiful bunny, that is the end of round 6.
